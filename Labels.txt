Quad_Cat

0 = HAHV - High Arousal High Valence - top right (like excited, proud, surprised, and happy) 
1 = LAHV - Low Arousal High Valence - bottom right (like relaxed, calm, glad, and satisfied)
2 = LALV - Low Arousal Low Valence - bottom left (like bored, sad, and disgusted)
3 = HALV - High Arousal Low Valence - top left (like anxious, frustrated, fear, and worry)
